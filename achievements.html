ACHIEVEMENTS

It brings us so much pride and honour to share that we the team Oxyheal have made our place in the top 25 in the very prestigious startup acceleration program hosted by iB Hubs startup school '19 and have also won three very important competitions which has helped us build in all the confidence we have on our team and on our product. The three of them being the NITK Ventures startup contest, Pragati 2020 and the In-Spire 2020.

iB Hubs Startup School 2019

A 4-week acceleration program, designed for the student entrepreneurs, to instil entrepreneurial fundamentals and accelerate a startup. Over 600 applications are sent out of which only 25 are selected and the Oxyheal team takes up much pride in stating that we have secured our place being one of those 25 and are proud alumni of the startup school.

NITK Ventures Startup Contest

It brings us immense joy to share that we the team of Oxyheal have backed 1st position amongst all the 21 teams participating. The competition was hosted in NITK by the college alumni.

PRAGATI 2020

Oxyheal team participated in the prestigious annual inter college technological Innovation Competition of STFIT-E-Cell where students from different disciplines compete with each other by giving out innovative projects which benefit the society and it brings us immense joy that we have been successful in winning the B-plan event.

In-Spire 2020

In-spire 2020 has come up with its first ever edition and we are proud to state that we were successful in bringing the laurel of the first position holders in the B-Plan event.

B-Plan Competition IIT Kharagpur

We are happy to announce that Oxyheal backed second position in the IIT Kharagpur B-Plan competition by the ecell IIT KGP held amidst the pandemic situation.

Vincenza MDI Gurgaon

Vincenza the annual B-plan competition held was held in the Management Development Institute Gurgaon and we are proud to announce that team Oxyheal stood first in the same. The overall experience was amazing for the team.

Ideonator, Shiv Nadar University

Ideonator is Business Competition under MOQSH 2020 organised by Shiv Nadar University (SNU), Greater Noida and we at Oxyheal are more than happy to bring home the first prize in this prestigious event.

Pratyay, IIM Shillong

Pratyay – Ideas that Inspire is Business Competition organised by Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Shillong and Oxyheal has set yet another benchmark by securing the first place in this amazing competition held online due to the lockdown scenario.

XIME B-plan competition

XIME was established in 1991 by a group of distinguished academicians under the leadership of Prof. J. Philip, former Director of IIM, Bangalore. It brings us so much pride to secure the first place at regionals(south zone) in its B-plan competition and are fully geared up for the upcoming nationals.

Oxyheal is a startup which aims at developing a medical device for the treatment of chronic wounds so as to reduce the chances of limb amputation in diabetic foot ulcer and vascular disease patients.
